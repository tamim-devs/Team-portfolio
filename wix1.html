Carolina Grazing Co. (Wix Website)

Carolina Grazing Co. is a premium grazing and charcuterie brand based in North Carolina, and I designed & developed their official website using Wix. The project focused on creating a luxury yet welcoming online presence that matches the elegance of their catering services. The website highlights: Elegant, image-driven design that showcases charcuterie boards and grazing tables with high-quality visuals. User-friendly navigation allowing visitors to easily explore services, pricing, and contact information. Mobile-first responsive design, ensuring smooth browsing on phones, tablets, and desktops. Booking & inquiry system integrated directly for customer convenience. Modern layout with subtle animations, reflecting a premium dining and lifestyle experience. This project demonstrates my ability to use Wix to craft professional, polished websites for service-based businesses, balancing both aesthetic and functionality.
